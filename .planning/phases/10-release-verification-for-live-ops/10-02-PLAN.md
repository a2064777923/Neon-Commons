<objective>

Purpose: once Plan 01 widens the suites, operators still need a readable and diagnosable release command. This plan keeps `verify:release` as the single source of truth, adds narrow rerun helpers for the new reliability surface, and makes stage output point at the exact live-ops and recovery gates that can fail.
Output: helper commands for targeted reruns plus a release runner whose stage output and failure guidance describe the widened Phase 10 release contract clearly.
